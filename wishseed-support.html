How to Use

Three steps to start
your dream journey

🌸
Step 01

Create a Wish List

Start your first dream

Tap the "+" button, give your dream a name, and choose a cover image. Whether it's travel, learning, or life goals — every beautiful wish begins here.

Step 02

Add Subtasks & Photos

Capture every step

Break down each wish into small tasks. Add up to 8 photos to document your progress — images speak louder than words.

Step 03

Generate & Share Timeline

Celebrate your growth

Once a wish is complete, generate a beautiful timeline image. Share it with friends or save it to your gallery — enjoy the joy of every seed blooming.
